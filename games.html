GAMES

OpenAlon focuses on approachable games with expressive characters, satisfying progression, and event-ready systems.

We design for delight at first touch and depth over time, giving players clear goals, meaningful growth, and reasons to come back.

GAME PILLARS

We think about games as living systems: progression, events, and worldbuilding should reinforce each other instead of competing for attention.

Live Events

Fresh moments should feel meaningful, not disposable. We use live content to create rhythm, surprise, and reasons to return.

Worldbuilding

Characters, destinations, and reward arcs should make every system feel part of a bigger world players want to inhabit.
